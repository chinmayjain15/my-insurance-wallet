# MyInsuranceWallet — Concept Note

## The Problem

Insurance exists to protect people at their most vulnerable moments — a sudden hospitalisation, a critical illness, or an untimely death. Yet at precisely those moments, the people who need to act on a policy often can't find it.

Policies are buried in emails, insurer portals, and physical folders. The insured person may be incapacitated. Their family may not even know what policies exist, let alone where to find them.

This is not a rare edge case. It is the norm.

---

## The Idea

**MyInsuranceWallet** is a simple, trusted space where you store your insurance policies and share them with the people who matter.

Think of it as a shared locker — not for you, but for the people who may need to step in on your behalf.

---

## How It Works

1. **You sign up** using your phone number.
2. **You upload your policies** — health, life, vehicle, term — organised by type.
3. **You add trusted contacts** — your spouse, parent, sibling, or a close friend.
4. **They get access** — when they log in, they can see exactly what you've shared with them.

That's it.

---

## Why This Is Different

| Where policies live today | What's missing |
|---|---|
| Email inbox | Hard to find, not shared |
| Insurer portal | Separate login per insurer, not shared |
| DigiLocker | Identity documents, not insurance-focused |
| Google Drive / WhatsApp | Unsorted, insecure, not purpose-built |

None of these give a trusted family member organised, on-demand access to your policies. MyInsuranceWallet does exactly that.

---

## Who It's For

- Anyone with at least one insurance policy in India
- Families where the primary earner holds all the policies
- Anyone who has thought: *"Does my family know what to do if something happens to me?"*

---

## What It Is Not

- Not a financial advisor or recommendation engine (yet)
- Not an insurer or broker
- Not a complex document management platform

It is a focused, lightweight tool that solves one problem well.

---

## The Guiding Principle

> Built for the moment when someone else needs to act on your behalf.

---

## What Comes Next

Once the core is stable and trusted by early users, future directions include:

- **Email extraction** — automatically pull policies from your inbox
- **Policy summaries** — plain-language breakdown of what's covered and what's not
- **Family document wallet** — extend beyond insurance to other critical documents
- **Marketplace** — connect users with insurers and new policy options
